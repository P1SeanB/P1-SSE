Preventive Maintenance Checklist
AC
Access Control
▶
Test all card readers and keypads
Verify mag-locks, strikes, and door hardware
Test REX devices and door hold-open timers
Verify fire-release / fail-safe integration
Audit credentials — remove stale users
Inspect wiring, controllers, power supplies
Check firmware / software update status
BA
Burglar Alarm
▶
Walk-test all motion detectors
Test all door/window contacts
Test panel, keypads, sirens, and strobes
Verify communicator: cellular + IP path
Load-test backup battery — replace if <80%
Check tampers and anti-masking zones
Update dispatch / contact list at central station
CC
CCTV / Cameras
▶
Clean lens and housing on all cameras
Verify field of view, focus, IR performance
Check NVR/DVR drives — SMART data and health
Confirm retention period is met (30-day standard)
Test remote access and VMS login
Review firmware updates — cameras and recorder
AV
A/V System
▶
Inspect all display mounts and cabling
Test audio zones and amplifier output levels
Verify control system operation and macros
Clean displays and speaker grilles
Test source switching and signal routing
Review firmware / driver update status

6
Response Time Commitments
Set the maximum commitments Company will hold for each call-out type. Triage is the acknowledgment window — the time from call receipt to a callback or ticket open by a qualified person. Onsite Deployment is the time from acknowledgment to a technician physically on site (where remote resolution is not possible). Defaults are pre-set based on the selected Service Tier and may be adjusted per customer.
Call-Out Type Triage (Acknowledgment) Onsite Deployment Trigger Conditions / Coverage
Regular Time Calls placed during business hours (M–F 7 am–5 pm), non-emergency conditions
After Hours Calls placed evenings, weekends, or observed holidays — non-critical
Emergency System down, life-safety failure, access breach, panel offline — any time
Custom Call-Outs
Add named response commitments outside the standard tiers (meeting support, lockdown response, etc.). Rate × hours is spread into the SLA rate and billing frequency, and each prints on the proposal as its own response and budget line.
